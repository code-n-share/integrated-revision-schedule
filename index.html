💡 Study Tips & Strategies

Maths Tips

  • Practice problems daily
  • Keep a mistakes notebook
  • Work through solutions step-by-step

Science Tips

  • Create labeled diagrams
  • Understand the 'why' behind processes
  • Use color coding

Geography Tips

  • Practice map reading
  • Create case study flashcards
  • Draw and label diagrams

Business Tips

  • Learn definitions precisely
  • Create comparison tables
  • Apply to real examples

General Strategies

  • Use Pomodoro technique
  • Create mind maps
  • Make flashcards

Exam Prep

  • Practice past papers
  • Get 7-8 hours sleep
  • Review weak areas
