# Integrated Revision Schedule - First Year

An interactive, persistent revision planner for tracking your study progress across 4 subjects: Maths, Science, Geography, and Business Studies.

## 🌟 Features

- **Interactive Checklist**: Check off topics as you complete them
- **Persistent Storage**: All changes are automatically saved to your browser's local storage
- **6-Week Schedule**: April 13 - May 18, 2026
- **4 Subjects**: 52 chapters total
  - Maths: 17 chapters
  - Science: 13 chapters
  - Geography: 6 chapters
  - Business Studies: 16 chapters
- **Weekly Progress Tracking**: See your progress at a glance
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **No Backend Required**: 100% client-side, runs completely offline

## 💾 Data Persistence

### How It Works
- All checkbox states are saved to your browser's **localStorage**
- Changes persist automatically without needing to save
- Each device/browser has its own copy of your progress

### Important Notes
- ✅ Data is saved automatically when you check/uncheck items
- ✅ Data persists even if you close your browser
- ✅ Data persists if you refresh the page
- ⚠️ Clearing browser cache/cookies will erase your progress
- ⚠️ Progress is device/browser specific (not synced across devices)

### Backup Your Progress
To backup your progress, you can:
1. Right-click → Inspect → Application → Local Storage
2. Look for "revisionSchedule" key
3. Copy the JSON data to save it
